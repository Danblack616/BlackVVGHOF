NES Advantage

Having this made me feel as close to playing at the arcade as I could without actually being there. The joystick and the big red buttons had that arcade feel and made this very intuitave to use.

PS1 Memory card

Until this beauty came into being you either had to write down passwords or hope that the game you were playing had a save function. Otherwise you were restarting at checkpoints or in some cases the beginning of the game. See below for a breakdown of the various save methods available at the time of it's release and judge for yourself

Save Method Advantages Disadvantages
Password Better than not saving at all Usually long and complicated
On Cart Battery Easy to Use If Battery Failed You Lost Your Save Data, Low Capacity
Memory Card Easy to Use, High Capacity, Portable Portability makes it Easier to Misplace

The Original Dual Shock

We didn't know it at the time but this would become the standard for all controllers going forward. The dual thumbstick design was revolutionary at the time pavinig the way for what games could do.
